Data Deletion & Account Removal

Last Updated: December 7, 2024

How to Request Account Deletion

You can request the deletion of your Onwa account and associated data through the following methods:

Method 1: In-App Deletion

  1. Open the Onwa app on your device
  2. Go to your Profile screen
  3. Tap on "Settings" or "Account Settings"
  4. Select "Delete Account" or "Remove Account"
  5. Follow the confirmation prompts
  6. Enter your password to confirm deletion

Method 2: Email Request

  1. Send an email to: onwa.app@outlook.com
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include your username or email address associated with your account
  4. State your request for account deletion
  5. We will respond within 30 days to confirm your request

How to Request Data Deletion (Without Deleting Account)

You can request deletion of specific data without deleting your entire account:

Delete Individual Content

  • Delete Diary Entries: Open any diary entry you created, tap the edit button, then select "Delete Entry"
  • Delete Buzzwords: Open any buzzword you created, tap the edit button, then select "Delete Buzzword"
  • Delete Comments: Tap the three dots next to any comment you made, then select "Delete"

Email Request for Specific Data Deletion

  1. Send an email to: onwa.app@outlook.com
  2. Use the subject line: "Data Deletion Request"
  3. Include your username or email address
  4. Specify which data you want deleted (e.g., "all my diary entries", "my profile picture", "my search history")
  5. We will process your request within 30 days

What Data is Deleted vs. Kept

Data That Will Be Deleted:

  • Account Information: Email address, username, password
  • Profile Data: Bio, profile pictures, personal information
  • User Content: All diary entries, buzzwords, comments, images, videos
  • Social Data: Followers, following lists, likes, interactions
  • Search History: Your in-app search queries
  • Usage Data: How you interacted with the app
  • Device Information: Device identifiers associated with your account

Data That May Be Retained:

  • Legal Records: Information required by law or for legal proceedings
  • Security Logs: Records of security incidents or violations (anonymized)
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated, anonymized usage statistics (no personal identifiers)
  • Backup Copies: Temporary backup copies for up to 30 days

Data Retention Periods

Account Deletion:

  • Immediate: Account becomes inactive and inaccessible
  • 7 days: Data is permanently deleted from active databases
  • 30 days: Backup copies are permanently deleted
  • 90 days: Any remaining cached data is cleared

Individual Content Deletion:

  • Immediate: Content becomes invisible to other users
  • 24 hours: Content is permanently deleted from active databases
  • 7 days: Backup copies are permanently deleted

Analytics and Logs:

  • 30 days: Personal usage logs are deleted
  • 1 year: Aggregated, anonymized analytics may be retained

Confirmation and Verification

After processing your deletion request, we will:

  • Send you a confirmation email within 30 days
  • Provide details of what was deleted
  • Confirm the deletion timeline
  • Offer assistance if you have any questions

Recovery Period

After requesting account deletion, you have a 7-day recovery period during which you can:

  • Cancel your deletion request by logging back into your account
  • Contact us to restore your account
  • Download your data before permanent deletion

Contact Information for Deletion Requests

For all deletion requests and questions about data removal:

  • Email: onwa.app@outlook.com
  • Response Time: Within 30 days
  • Verification Required: We may ask you to verify your identity
  • Processing Time: 7-30 days depending on request type

Additional Rights

In addition to deletion, you also have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of all your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information

  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data
